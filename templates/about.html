About Catanduanes Connect

Connecting communities, creating opportunities. Our mission is to bridge the gap between job seekers, businesses, and service providers in Catanduanes.

Our Mission

To empower the people of Catanduanes by providing a comprehensive platform that connects job opportunities, local businesses, and professional services. We aim to foster economic growth and create sustainable employment opportunities for our community.

Our Vision

To become the leading digital platform for employment and business opportunities in Catanduanes, contributing to the province's economic development and helping build a thriving local economy.

What We Offer

Comprehensive solutions for our community

Job Portal

Find and apply for local job opportunities. Connect with employers who value local talent and skills.

Business Directory

Discover and support local businesses. Find verified companies with reviews and ratings from the community.

Service Marketplace

Find professional services or offer your own expertise. Connect with skilled professionals in your area.

Real-time Chat

Get instant assistance through our AI-powered chatbot and connect directly with service providers.

Reviews & Ratings

Make informed decisions with community-driven feedback and transparent rating systems.

Mobile Friendly

Access all features on any device. Our platform is fully optimized for mobile and desktop use.
